Terms and Conditions of TNC Cuban Realm and Discord

Discord rules

Welcome to the Team Ninja Craft community Hangout, have fun, chat, make friends, and enjoy minecraft with others, because minecraft is better together. Like in all Ninja Training we do expect that you follow the Ninja Code to ensure everyone has a great experience here.

  1. Abide by Discord's "Terms of Service and Community Guidelines": you must be 13 years or older to use Discord. ----------- Here is a link to such https://discordapp.com/guidelines
  2. Respect: be cool, be civil; it's okay to have different opinions but respect everyone's right to have theirs as well.

  3. No Hate: no racism, sexism, anti-LGBQT, or otherwise offensive content. There is zero tolerance for hate speech, and will result in a permanent ban!

  4. Use appropriate channels:
    • dojo-hangout - general chat
    • shared-minecraft - minecraft builds and redstone
    • mc-promo - minecraft videos/minecraft community member live streams
    • meme_central - memes (no XP gained)

    5. Do not use "@here" or "@everyone". Just don't do it.
  5. No advertising other sites or discord servers.

  6. Any questions, or problems ask an Admin or Moderator, we have a great team always willing to help.

    7. Thank you for supporting Team Ninja Craft and have fun earning your belts in the Dojo. @here VOICE CHANNELS


  1. No voice chat surfing or switching channels repeatedly.

  2. No annoying, loud or high pitch noises.

  3. Reduce the amount of background noise, if possible. Resort to push to talk in your settings to reduce the issue.

Note: Rules are subject to change without notice, and if broken could result in a ban, which is permanent; all decisions are final.


Realm Rules

  1. Discord rules apply here as well, use common sense
  2. Be nice, fair and respectful to your fellow realm members and their builds.
  3. There will be absolutely no stealing, griefing, or duping of any kind under any circumstance.
  4. We love our server and want to keep it running great, so no building beyond the build border.
  5. No Mega Builds or Farms unless authorized by a Moderator. #NoLag
  6. If a creeper blows up fix the damage ASAP, and put a sign to message owner explaining what happened
  7. If any issues arise, tag or DM the moderators on discord immediately

      8. Shops:
    • Your shop must have a set “theme” or group of items it sells. EX. Gold sells Gold Ingots, Gold Carrots, Golden Apple ect... (if your not sure ask)
    • You can sell items, animals, services, or whatever you would like to.
    • You can Sell the same things as other players.
    • All rules are subject to change, and we reserve the right to remove any shop and the contents if necessary.
